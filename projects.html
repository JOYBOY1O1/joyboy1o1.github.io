Augmented Reality

Augmented Reality (AR) is an innovative technology that enriches the real-world environment by superimposing digital elements, such as graphics, audio, or information, onto the user's view. By blending the physical and virtual worlds, AR enhances the user's perception and interaction with the surroundings.

Multi Media visualizer

The Augmented Reality Media Viewer project aims to develop a cutting-edge application that revolutionizes the way media is consumed. By leveraging the power of Unity and Vuforia, the project seeks to create an immersive and interactive multi-media-watching experience.

The viewer will utilize augmented reality technology to overlay virtual content onto the real world, blurring the line between fiction and reality. Users will have the freedom to enjoy movies in a personalized and on-demand manner, transcending the limitations of traditional theaters. The project's primary objective is to showcase the potential of AR in transforming the entertainment industry, while providing a unique and captivating experience to users.

The project successfully integrates augmented reality features using Unity and Vuforia. It demonstrates the ability to play and pause videos based on virtual button interactions. The code can be expanded and customized to create more complex augmented reality experiences, combining real-world objects with interactive virtual elements.

Content Based recommendation system

A Content-Based Recommendation System is a type of recommendation system that suggests items to users based on the characteristics and features of the items themselves. It analyzes the content or attributes of the items that users have interacted with in the past and then recommends similar items.

Content-Based Recommendation Systems are commonly used in various applications, such as recommending movies, books, music, and news articles, where the characteristics of the items play a significant role in user preferences. By leveraging item attributes and user interactions, these systems offer personalized and relevant recommendations to users, enhancing their overall experience and engagement.

I have made a movie recommendation system that suggests movies to users based on their preferences and behavior. It aims to provide personalized movie recommendations, helping users discover films they are likely to enjoy. Movie recommendation systems use various algorithms and techniques, and two popular approaches are Collaborative Filtering and Content-Based Filtering.
